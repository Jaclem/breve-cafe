Breve Cafe offers its customers the best tasting coffee beverages in the area.
This is achieved by using high-quality ingredients and strictly
following preparation guidelines. Along with the espresso drinks, brewed coffee
and teas, as well as some refreshment beverages, are sold in the coffee bar.
Breve Cafe will also offer its clients pastries, small salads and sandwiches.
For the gourmet clientele that prefers to prepare its coffee at home, Breve Cafe
will also be selling coffee beans.
